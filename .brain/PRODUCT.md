# Product

Sapiens is an educational exploration game where players learn by entering interactive worlds, investigating evidence, making decisions, and observing consequences.

## Vision

Build a shared exploration universe spanning history.

Eventually reach: science, civics, geography, culture, and engineering. But will likely be scoped outside this MVP.

## Experience

- Begin with short text-based scenarios.
- Grow into visual novels and approachable 2D open-world hubs.
- Use real evidence and clearly label uncertainty, interpretation, and dramatization.
- Reward curiosity with knowledge, field notes, reputation, tools, and meaningful collectibles—not just quiz scores.

## Initial scope

One small, self-contained world with a few roles, characters, interactable objects, decisions, source-backed content, a journal, and persistent state.

## Platform

Web-first, desktop-friendly, and progressively responsive for tablets/mobile; package as a PWA when useful.
